    OUR Models



    Volvo V40


    The Volvo V40 is the compact hatchback that’s perfectly suited for life in the city. With agile handling, interior versatility and smart technology to make your life easier, the V40 is the perfect companion as you start your journey.City Weave upholstery continues the urban Scandinavian look of the V40. The frameless rear-view mirror and optional panoramic glass roof create a spacious sanctuary, connecting you with the outside – even while you’re inside.




    S90


    Introducing the elegant and confident Volvo S90. Combining exquisite, natural materials with sophisticated, intuitive technology, this is the next generation of Volvo Cars.Elegant, uncluttered and crafted from beautiful, natural materials – the S90 is a luxury sedan like no other. A calm, rewarding interior, combined with intuitive technology and filled with natural light, the S90 is an oasis of seclusion. This is the executive sedan, redefined.

    XC90

    x
    Relax, think and connect with the world on your own terms in the XC90 and discover a large, family SUV that puts you at complete ease. Inside, an uncluttered cabin crafted from the finest natural materials combines with technology that is intuitive to use. On the move, engines that are powerful, quiet and efficient allow for smooth progress. This luxurious, minimalist aesthetic is the essence of modern Scandinavian design. This is our idea of luxury.

    Volvo Bus 9400

    There are passengers who settle for nothing than the best. In the BS-IV compliant Volvo 9400, you can specify the luxurious interior for an even more personal and comfortable experience. Luxury comes standard. The exterior design signals a premium experience. Inside it is confirmed with high-quality materials, theatre floor and our top-level seats with ergonomic design.
